Instructions

This is a particle life simulation where different colored particles interact according to attraction and repulsion rules. Use the buttons above to control the simulation.

  • Presets: Choose from different simulation presets with unique behaviors
  • Toggle Wrap-Around: Make particles wrap around screen edges
  • Re-seed Particles: Keep rules but randomize particle positions
  • Slow Motion: Hold to slow down the simulation
  • Print Parameters: Show the current simulation parameters
  • Mouse: Click on a particle to follow it, right-click to stop following
  • Zoom: Use the zoom buttons to zoom in/out
